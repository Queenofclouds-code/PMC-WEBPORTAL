About PMC

Pune Municipal Corporation (PMC) is committed to enhancing the quality of civic life, improving infrastructure, and providing transparent and efficient public services.

Goals of PMC (2025)

  • Improve waste management & recycling processes
  • Enhance water supply & sewage systems
  • Promote digital governance & transparency
  • Develop smarter, sustainable infrastructure
  • Strengthen citizen grievance redressal systems

Projects Completed Till 2025

  • Smart Street Light Upgrade
  • Riverfront Beautification Drive
  • Expansion of Geo-Tagged Public Toilets
  • Digital Complaint Management System
  • Advanced Waste Processing Plants

PMC Mission Statement

“A cleaner, greener, smarter Pune — where development meets sustainability, and citizens come first.”
